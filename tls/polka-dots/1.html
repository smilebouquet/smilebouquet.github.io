Youth is the Colour of Summer 1

Early July, after school

Tetora: Hinata-kun, can I stop watering now?

Hinata: Yup! Some veggies don't do well with too much water. You've given them more than enough~

Annnd that's all for today! Thanks to you, I finished watering early ♪

Tetora: Only cuz you called out like, "Tetsu-kun, good timing!" and asked for my help.

Hinata: I'm sorry~! I happened to see you passing by ♪

Tetora: Don't sweat it, really. You would've gotten heatstroke if you watered the whole field by yourself in this heat.

Hinata: True. I'm thinking of getting one of those farmer's hats just for the summer.

Tetora: Oh, you're going all out... Well, I'm always happy to help, y'know.

Summer's just begun, so the heat's only gonna get worse.

Hinata: Thanks ♪ We started harvesting a bunch of summer veggies, so I'll share some of the yummy ones with you.

Tetora: Ahaha, can't wait ♪

...Geez, the watering didn't even take that long, but I'm sweating buckets.

Hinata: Yeah, gotta wipe it off and rehydrate. Wanna get something cold from the cafeteria?

Tetora: Hmm, I guess that works, but what if we stopped by someplace on the way home instead?

Narukami-senpai told me about this new shaved ice place that's pretty good the other day.

Hinata: Shaved ice? That totally screams summer, I'm in ♪

Where is it?

Tetora: Near downtown, I think? She sent me all the details, so I can take us there no sweat ♪

Hinata: Great, let's put away the watering cans and head out! Our shaved ice awaits!

Tetora: Ossu! I'll start thinking about what flavour to get!

(A little later)

Tetora: Mmm! This is so tasty, Hinata:-kun!

I thought so hard about it, but you can't go wrong with red bean and kuromitsu!

Hinata: No way, I bet my milk-shaved ice with strawberries and tomatoes is just as amazing! You'd see what I mean if you tried some!

...Sooo, gimme a bite of yours♪ I'll give you two of mine♪

Tetora: I'm good with just one. It's not fair if only I get to have two.

Hinata: Ehh~? But you didn't let me pay for your shaved ice earlier!

I just wanna thank you for helping me...

Tetora: Urgh... I'll take one *big* bite. I need you to be cool with that.

Okay, here you go.

Hinata: Grrr, fine. Here's some of mine...♪

Tetora: ...Mmph!

Hinata: What? Did my shaved ice knock your socks off?

Tetora: Kind of. But mainly I spotted a poster advertising a summer festival. Look, it's that one.

Hinata: Ohh, I see it. Feels like rainy season just ended, but I guess that means it's festival season now.

Tetsu-kun, do you have any festival memories?

Tetora: Festival memories? I guess the Shooting Star Festival last year counts...[1]

I think I got to eat shaved ice with Anego back then, too~

We ran into a bit of trouble before the live, but I still had fun. I'm pretty proud of the things I did back then.

...Oh, fun fact: even as a kid, I never got to go to that many–

Hinata: AHHHHH!

Tetora: W-What? Why'd you scream?

Hinata: Tetsu-kun, read carefully! This festival is happening today!

Tetora: Whoa, you're right! I was so distracted by all the pictures that I didn't notice.

Hinata: ...Today, huh.

Tetora: What about it?

Hinata: Nothing~. Just that I've got no work for today... Or anything to do at the office... Or any reason to go home yet...

Tetora: Me neither. It's almost like we were meant to discover this poster...

Tetora & Hinata: ......

Tetora: Kinda makes you wanna check it out, doesn't it♪

Hinata: I know, right? All right, let's go to this summer festival!

I'll snap a photo so we don't forget where it is. Click...☆

Tetora: We should send it to everyone. I'll put it in the second years group chat♪

Hinata: Sending to Yuuta-kun... There. Who else?

Tetora: I was thinking of sending it to our senpais, but they might be too busy.

Eh, if they can't go we'll just buy them dango or something♪

Hinata: Fufu. I can't wait to see what kind of stalls are gonna be there!

Tetora: Yakisoba, grilled squid, corn on the cob... Just thinking about it is making my stomach growl!

Hinata: Ooh, what about candied apples and other sweets? I heard that they sell other candied fruits nowadays, too.

Wouldn't it be cute if Yuuta-kun and I ate candied fruits together? Of course, I'm happy to eat whatever he wants even if it's not candied fruit...!

Tetora: Haha, I hope you two make some nice memories♪

I'm gonna enjoy this festival as much as I can, too!
