About this website

hello, and welcome! i made this website to host all my translations.

currently, i'm focusing on translating enstars songs! i really enjoy reading matsui youhei and codama saori's lyrics, so their work will make up the majority of what i post. i occasionally translate enstars stories as well (hinata focus), not to mention songs/stories outside of enstars. i'd like to post my own translations of certain prsk stories too someday :)

if you catch any mistakes in my translations (e.g. typos, grammatical errors or mistranslations), send me an ask and i will fix it as soon i can.
